How to Play:

* Each player will take turns rolling a pair of die as many times as they wish.

* You'll accrue points equal to the roll values.

* Click HOLD to store the points from your CURRENT SCORE to your TOTAL SCORE.

* If you roll a "1", you'll lose all of the points in your CURRENT SCORE and your turn ends.

* If you roll two "6"s in a row, you'll lose both your TOTAL SCORE and CURRENT SCORE and your turn will end, so be careful!.

* The first person to get their TOTAL SCORE to at least 100 wins!
